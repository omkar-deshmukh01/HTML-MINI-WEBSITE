WHAT IS CHAT GPT

ChatGPT is a generative artificial intelligence chatbot developed by OpenAI. Since its initial
release in November 2022, it has evolved into a versatile tool capable of processing and generating text, audio, and images.

CORE CONCEPT

  • What does "GPT" stand for?,It stands for Generative Pre-trained Transformer .

  • Generative: It creates new content (text, code, images, etc.) rather than just retrieving existing data.

  • Pre-trained: It has been trained on a massive dataset of text from the internet to recognize patterns in human language.

  • Transformer: This refers to the underlying neural network architecture that allows the model to understand the context and relationships between words in a sequence.

KEY CAPIBILITIES

  1. Research & Analysis: It can perform multi-step research by accessing online sources, comparing viewpoints, and providing summarized findings with citations.

  2. Multimodel Interaction: You can interact with it using text, voice, and images. It can analyze PDFs, spreadsheets, handwritten notes, and photographs simultaneously.

  3. Coding Assistance: It is widely used by developers to write, debug, explain, and optimize computer code, including the use of "agentic" modes that can perform tasks independently.

  4. Personalization: With its "Memory" feature, ChatGPT can remember your preferences, preferred communication styles, and recurring projects across different sessions.

  5. Content Generation: Beyond text, it can generate high-quality images, compose music, write scripts, and even simulate interactive environments like text-based games.
